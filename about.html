About TRANSLARE

TRANSLARE's localization expertise spans more than 2 decades.

TRANSLARE has 24 years of localization and translation experience, delivering precise and nuanced translation services to help our clients internationalize their products and services. Our staff of native linguists translates over 12 million words into European, Middle Eastern, Asian, and South American languages annually in 30 countries and 90 language pairs.

Our diversified and talented workforce and partners help us provide optimal services to our customers.

Established in Egypt, the geographic center of the Arab world, TRANSLARE has grown into a preeminent localization business serving customers across the world with expert, high-quality translations.

Our team of certified native translators has the skills and knowledge to expertly and methodically meet the demands of even your most picky clients.

Who are we ?

A team of native and seasoned linguists, project managers and sales personnel, with the right set of qualifications and expertise to help you find your niche in the global market. We are your go-to source to get the highest quality of service.
