Where Expertise Meets Excellence

Since 2011, Megalocalize has offered a broad range of services, from translation and localization to technical writing, software testing, and market research. Our Vision and Mission

Based in Egypt, the heartland of the Middle East, Megalocalize aims to be the premier gateway for international businesses looking to expand into the Middle East and Africa. Our vision is to establish our company as the go-to resource for organizations seeking growth in this dynamic region. Our mission is to facilitate the expansion of global enterprises while serving as a bridge connecting the Middle Eastern and African markets with the rest of the world.

Armed with a team of certified native translators, we possess the expertise and knowledge necessary to meet the unique demands of our clients in a professional and systematic manner. Our goal is to help you navigate the complexities of localization and translation, driving profitability in this rich and diverse market.
