MTPE or Human Translation

Technology improves every year and the quality of translation it offers is constantly rising which makes the debate around Machine translation vs Human translation is always controversial.

As a matter of fact, Machine translation is cheaper, faster, and accessible. However, it has very low quality, unnatural sense, misconceptions, and potential errors that cost a lot.

As for human translation, it is much more effective and accurate yet it costs a lot. So, combining both in MTPE is the optimum option for businesses who are looking for affordable translation solutions with higher quality and more accuracy than Machine translation.

Nevertheless, MTPE and full human translation do have differences that you better consider before opting for what is best fit for your business.

We can summarize the differences between both in a matter of cons & pros as follows:

MTPE

Pros :

  • Affordable price
  • Higher quality than machine translation
  • Higher clarity
  • Improved flow
  • Greater cultural sensitivity

Cons :

  • Slower than complete Machine Translation
  • Potentially lower quality than full Human Translation
  • The MT output may include bad and meaningless text that the translator cannot understand, leading to potential misunderstanding on the translator’s side, yet it was totally not their fault.
  • Some linguistic nuances can be lost in the process.
  • Inefficient for rare languages.

Human translation

Pros:

  • Recognition of human aspects & cultural aspects in translation
  • Convey deeper layers of meaning
  • High quality
  • Only human translators can deliver high-quality translations for complex subjects
  • Translating any language pairs

Cons:

  • Higher cost
  • More costly

It is better to use a human translation for the following purposes:

  • Documents of high importance
  • Documents of high visibility
  • Documents of high quality
  • Documents of human intelligence
  • Documents of cultural sensitivity
