Translation phases:

At TRANSLARE, we abide by the ISO standards, so we provide a TEP process followed by LQA procedures to ensure higher quality translations.

TEP

It is a strategic process used to ensure the utmost accuracy and convey the original meaning.

In search of quality translation services, you should look for translation providers who run the three-step process to avoid the risk of delay or even additional cost to correct unnecessary translation mistakes

LQA

After the TEP process, we provide the Linguistic Quality Assurance process by which linguists review translations by using a set of methodologies to verify the precision of a translation for its cultural accuracy, linguistic phrasing, terminology, adherence to guidelines, formatting, and style to detect if they contain any objective errors and to assure adequate quality of the provided translation.

The LQA team can meet any language in the Middle East & Africa and can meet any QA requirements of the client’s request.
