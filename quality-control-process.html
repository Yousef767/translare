Quality control process

Resource selection

We choose native resources of the target language with a university degree in the translation field. They perform tests in their area of expertise to identify the area they are best for. They are trained and constantly reviewed with regular evaluation to ensure development. Our resources acquire translation certificates from different organizations.

Project Management

We have dedicated project managers who act as a connection point between the client and the production. They put action and operational plan, and they make sure that the production team abides by all the guidelines and implements all the requests to meet client satisfaction. They keep follow-up and consultancy with the client to ensure services development as well.

Project Cycle

We follow an inclusive project cycle that starts with materials analysis and preparation passing by resource selection, TEP process, and Formatting, and ends with several QA checks to achieve the highest quality standard, eliminate any possible errors, and make the project content look and feel like the original.

Three phases of translation

We follow the TEP process to make sure that every text is translated, reviewed, and proofread to eliminate all errors and mistakes related to context, culture, terminology, structure, or grammar & punctuation. The proofreading phase gives approval for the files that they are error-free.

SME check

We assign the files to subject matter experts who have extensive knowledge in the requested field to ensure that the technical information and the used terminology are accurate and precise. They are responsible for continuous development in the field to achieve a sufficient level of professionalism.

Modern technologies

Our team is experienced in all popular translation and localization software and tools. Team leads and project managers propose guides and instructions to explain the working system of the tools and software. We also develop and add any non-popular tool, modern or old versions, to meet any requirements.

QA check

We provide both human & automated QA checks to ensure optimum quality and highest productivity. The QC team checks the files and simulates as a reader to finally give final approval to the project linguistically, culturally, and technically.

Follow-up system

We have a priority to satisfy our clients' needs, our process continues after delivery by our PMs checking for any updates and queries on previous projects or coming requests. We are willing to add new fields and services if they serve our client's objectives and make sure that we are a successful partner.
