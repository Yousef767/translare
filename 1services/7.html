To uphold our standards of excellence, we implement a strict selection process for both in-house and freelance translators, which includes:

  • Having at least four years of translation and localization experience
  • Specialization in three or more translation areas, with strong general skills
  • Expertise in relevant fields and proficiency in Computer-Assisted Translation (CAT) tools
