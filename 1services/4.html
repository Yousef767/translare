The Importance of Terminology Management

Efficient terminology management is vital for ensuring the quality and consistency of the final translated output. By implementing robust terminology practices, organizations can significantly reduce costs while accelerating timelines. Effective management minimizes unnecessary revisions, eliminates redundant review and quality assurance phases, and ultimately accelerates project turnaround times. Furthermore, maintaining consistent terminology within departments promotes mutual understanding among employees, fostering a smoother collaboration.
