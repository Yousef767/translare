Elevating Translations into Professional Presentations

At Megalocalize, our Desktop Publishing (DTP) services are designed to ensure that translated and localized content is meticulously formatted, preserving the original document's structure, layout, and visual appeal. DTP serves as the final touch in preparing content for print, involving various processes to enhance the presentation of translated materials. Utilizing industry-leading software such as Adobe InDesign and QuarkXPress, we create high-quality layouts for a wide array of print documents, including books, magazines, and flyers. Our comprehensive DTP process includes:

  • Typesetting and text formatting
  • Image and graphic resizing
  • File format conversion
  • Correcting alignment, orientation, and layout
  • Checking consistency in headers, footers, and page numbers
  • Creation of indices and Tables of Contents
Rigorous final quality control

We also leverage graphic design tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to ensure that your images and content are visually appealing and professional. Our team ensures the final product maintains aesthetic integrity and adheres to regional formatting standards for consistent professionalism.

Arabic DTP Services

Arabic Desktop Publishing presents distinctive challenges due to its right-to-left (RTL) script, necessitating adjustments such as layout flipping, RTL alignment, and proper formatting of numbers. Utilizing specific Arabic fonts is essential for achieving smooth readability and preventing text distortion. At Megalocalize, we excel in delivering high-quality Arabic DTP services, collaborating with global companies to ensure their content is flawlessly formatted.
