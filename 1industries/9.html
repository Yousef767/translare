Medical Devices

Megalocalize specializes in providing translation services for leading medical device manufacturers worldwide. Our team consists of professionals with a minimum of five years of experience in the translation of medical device documentation, ensuring a deep understanding of technical specifications and industry-specific terminology.

The translation of medical devices is a highly specialized area that requires a unique blend of technical knowledge and extensive experience. Accuracy is paramount, as any misinterpretation can lead to improper device use and jeopardize patient safety. This principle extends to translating equipment functionalities and graphical user interfaces (GUIs), where precise localization is necessary to guarantee that caregivers and patients can perform the correct actions.

At Megalocalize, we are committed to delivering precise and reliable translations, ensuring that your medical devices are effectively communicated in every market.

We offer translation services for a wide range of medical device materials, including:

  • Anesthesia delivery and ventilation equipment
  • Catheters, guide wires, and surgical instruments
  • Dentistry and diagnostic equipment
  • ECG systems and patient monitoring devices
  • Cardiac rhythm management devices
  • Endoscopy and radiology/ultrasound imaging devices
  • Magnetic resonance imaging devices
  • User manuals for medical stretchers and patient chairs
  • Sterilization and infection control systems
